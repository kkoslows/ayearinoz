Promises

In order to create consistency and a story you can follow, we've created a set of promises to you as you follow our year-long journey.

  1. We will log our experiences at least once a week
  2. We will your log questions. Email:us@holtandkristin.com
  3. We will log any experience, good and bad, happy and sad
  4. We will tell you about our day-to-day, weekends, and special trips
  5. We will take photos or paint a picture with our descriptions so you can see what we see
  6. We will NOT relocate back to the US within a year (although we'll visit for xmas and weddings)
